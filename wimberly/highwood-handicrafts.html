Highwood Handicrafts

Welcome to Highwood Handicrafts! My booth at Wimberly Market Days consist of handmade wooden decorations that have a country antique feel to them. I use whatever type of wood I can get my hands on from scrap wood to free wooden pallets I get from whoever is getting rid of them. I design and make them by myself along with my wife. I have made many different sizes and shapes according to whatever design we come up with. I also do custom ones if someone has something particular in mind.

This wooden barn block is 15"x15" and is made out of pine pallets.

This wooden barn quilt block is 17"x17" and is made out of oak and pine.

This wooden barn quilt block is 7"x7" and is made out of alder and poplar.

This charcurterie board is 18"x12" and is made out of sweet gum.
