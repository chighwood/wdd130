What is Wimberly Market Days?

Wimberly Market Days is one of the biggest outdoor markets in Texas. It is located in Wimberly, Texas just south of Austin. It has 490 booths filled with anything you could imagine. There are homemade goods including stained glass, wood projects, quilts, dog bandanas, hand painted canvas's, and even little wooden trains. Lots of the booths consist of goods like old decor, collectables, cooking utensils, and antique items. One of the things you'll see the most is all of the Texas decor. From signs, to floor mats, pictures, and espically Texas shaped wall hangings. If you can think of it, they probalbly have it.

There are so many cool items to buy. There are things for birthday gifts, wedding gifts, and even presents for little kids. There is something there for everyone and anyone who wants to be apart of a small-town market. Bring the kids, your dog, or anyone else willing to go. It is a place you should visit at least once in your lifetime if the old country scene is your type of place to be. The people there are so nice and friendly and happy to have visitors even if you are just looking around.
